About Us

Founded in 1994, Govind Ashramshala has been a beacon of quality education and holistic development for students in the region. The school operates under the esteemed guidance of the Dnyanganga Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, an educational trust committed to shaping young minds through academic excellence and value-based learning.
At the heart of Govind Ashramshala is a team of dedicated leaders and educators who strive to create an environment where every child is encouraged to learn, grow, and succeed.
Our Leadership:

Chairman: Adv. Ram Hari Rupanawar

A respected Ex-MLC, active member of the Congress Party, and a practicing Advocate, Mr. Rupanawar brings a wealth of experience and a vision for social upliftment through education. Under his dynamic leadership, the school has flourished as a hub for academic growth and community development.

Secretary: Mr. Adinath Rupanawar

A skilled Ayurvedic professional and dedicated Congress Party member, Mr. Rupanawar plays a key role in the administration and strategic development of the institution. His commitment to service and education continues to inspire progress at Govind Ashramshala.

Headmaster: Mr. Mahamad Kasim Shaikh

With a passion for teaching and a deep commitment to student development, Mr. Shaikh leads the academic team with integrity and dedication. His leadership ensures a disciplined, inclusive, and enriching learning environment for all students.
